How to send the signing files
⚠️ Gmail blocks HTML attachments — and Google Drive only shows raw code.
Use Netlify Drop: free, instant, no account needed.
3 steps per signatory:
1. Go to app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag & drop the sign-xxx.html file onto the page
3. Copy the generated link → paste it in your email to the signatory
✅ The link opens a real interactive signing page — works on phone & computer, no app needed.
